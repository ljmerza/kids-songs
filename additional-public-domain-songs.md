## Notes
- All songs listed are in the public domain in most countries
- Songs published before 1928 in the US are generally in the public domain
- Traditional folk songs and nursery rhymes are typically public domain
- Always verify copyright status before commercial use
- Many of these songs have simple, repetitive melodies perfect for beginner guitar
- Prioritize songs with melodies that stay within a 5-fret range for beginners
