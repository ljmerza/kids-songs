## Notes
- Many of these songs share similar melodies (e.g., ABC Song, Twinkle Twinkle, and Baa Baa Black Sheep all use the same tune)
- Songs are listed roughly in order of popularity and ease of guitar tablature arrangement
- Simple, repetitive melodies work best for beginner guitar learners
